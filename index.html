The T-Level is a 2-year programme that offers an attractive alternative to A Levels and Apprenticeships. The T-Level in Digital Production Design and Development offers a combination of classroom theory, practical learning, and a minimum of 315 hours of industry placements with an employer to ensure you have real experience in the workplace.
